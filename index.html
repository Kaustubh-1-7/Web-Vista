Frequently Asked Questions

What is YourQuizMaster?

YourQuizMaster is a comprehensive platform that allows users to create, customize, and take quizzes across various subjects, enhancing the learning experience.

How can I create a quiz?

Simply navigate to the "Create Your Own Quiz" section, choose your subject, and follow the step-by-step instructions to design your quiz.

What features are included in the Pro Plan?

The Pro Plan offers up to 100 quiz creations, AI support, access to leaderboards, mentor support, and exclusive 3D video content to enhance your learning journey.

Is there a free version available?

Yes, we offer a basic free version that allows limited quiz creations and access to our study materials. Upgrade to Premium for more features.

How do I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team through the "Contact" link in the navbar or by emailing support@yourquizmaster.com.
